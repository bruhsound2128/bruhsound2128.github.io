How to Reach Solapur

By Road

Connected to Pune (250 km), Hyderabad (300 km), Mumbai (400 km), Bengaluru (380 km). Regular buses operate.

By Rail

Solapur Railway Station is a major junction connecting Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Bengaluru.

By Air

Solapur has a small airport with limited flights. Nearest major airports: Pune International and Hyderabad International.

Local Transport

  • Auto rickshaws
  • Taxis
  • City buses
