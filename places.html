Siddheshwar Temple

Dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar, a form of Lord Shiva, the temple is located near Siddheshwar Lake in the center of the city. Thousands of devotees visit daily, especially during the Siddheshwar Yatra in January.

Pandharpur

Located 70 km from Solapur, Pandharpur is famous for the Vitthal Rukmini Temple. Millions of devotees join the Wari pilgrimage during Ashadhi and Kartiki Ekadashi festivals.

Akkalkot

About 40 km from Solapur, Akkalkot is known for the temple of Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj. The peaceful temple atmosphere attracts visitors nationwide.

Solapur Fort

Also called Bhuikot Fort, it was built during the Bahmani Sultanate and later ruled by various dynasties. It reflects medieval Indian military architecture.

Tuljapur

Tuljapur is another important pilgrimage destination near Solapur, famous for the Tulja Bhavani Temple dedicated to Goddess Bhavani.
